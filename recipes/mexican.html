Microwave Mexican Rice

This no-fuss microwave Mexican rice is quick and simple. Using instant rice, it comes together in a flash, and goes great with tacos, burritos, and enchiladas.

Ingredients:

  • Instant Rice
  • Water
  • Picante Sauce
  • Chopped green chiles
  • Envelop taco seasoning

Steps:

  1. Place minute rice, water, picante sauce, green chiles, and taco seasoning in a large microwave-safe baking dish. Stir until evenly combined.
  2. Microwave, uncovered, on High for 8 minutes. Let sit for 5 minutes to absorb liquid. Fluff with a fork and serve.
